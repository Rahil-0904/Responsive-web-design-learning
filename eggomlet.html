Simple Egg Omlet Receipe

Here we gonna learn how to prepare a Omlet under 3 Min.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • Salt, pepper
  • Pan, Bowl, a spatula
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. first heat the pan in mid flames and pour some oil and distribute evenly in pan.
  2. Break all three eggs and mix them in a bowl.
  3. add some salt & pepper and pour it inside pan evenly.
  4. wait two min to cook until omlet turns light brown, pick with spatula in a plate & enjoy your Omley.
